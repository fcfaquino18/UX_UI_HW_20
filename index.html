Odyseek Mobile App Design

UX/UI Designer

Introduction


For this project, I designed an app to help travelers streamline their trip planning process. I was inspired to create this app based off my own frustrations in trying to create itinerary while staying on budget, and orchestrating the perfect trip for all involved.

Overview


The Problem

Trip planning, especially when traveling in a group, has always been challenging, from coordinating with all people involved, setting the itinerary and budgeting. Everyone have their own preferences causing overwhelming amount of information to make every decision. How might we improve their planning process and orchestrate a perfect trip?


The Goal

Design a mobile solution for planning itinerary, discovering sight information and recommendations, keeping track of expenses and documenting trips in one platform for quick access.


My Role

I was the sole researcher and designer on this project. As someone who travels often, I felt passionate about the concept, and wanted to address the needs by crafting visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that facilitate smooth navigation and interaction, while honing my skills in UX/UI.

Research


Interviews

Five interviews and thirty surveys were conducted with travelers who usually go on a group trip every year, in order to understand what steps users take, their preferences, challenges and expectations when planning a trip.

Conclusion

It was a very challenging project, but it was an incredible learning experience. Parts of it were bit of a trial by fire, but it was very rewarding in the end, and I gained new valuable knowledge and skills.

I’d like to continue developing the app and work on larger scale version of this project involving exciting additional features. I’m proud of the product I’ve created and solution I’ve delivered, and I’m looking forward to its progress in the future!
